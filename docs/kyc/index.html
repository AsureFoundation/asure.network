TGE - Token Generation Event

The ASURE TGE will happen in two rounds. The ﬁrst round will take place in mid 2019. The second round will happen later in 2019. Tokens will be ERC20 / ERC223 compatible and limited in supply.

ASR Token

The token will be used for On-Chain Governance and by Network Validators as well as Service Providers to participate as stakers in the proof-of-stake consensus mechanisms of the Asure network.
